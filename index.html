Lab 3

Exploring around VIETNAM

  • Province

    An Giang

    A pretty place in the south with floating markets and lots of green rice fields.

  • City

    Da Nang

    A cool city by the beach with big mountains nearby and bridges that look like dragons.

  • City

    Ha Long

    Where you can see lots of tall rocks sticking out of the sea, it's like a fairy tale.

  • Capital City of Vietnam

    Ha Noi

    The busy capital city with a big lake in the middle and old streets where you can find yummy food.

  • City

    Hai Phong

    A city by the water with old buildings from a long time ago and a ferry to a beautiful island.

  • City

    Ho Chi Minh (or Saigon)

    A well-known big city where there are tall buildings, museums about the war, and markets with lots of things to buy.

  • City

    Hoi An

    A cute old town with colorful lanterns hanging everywhere.

  • City

    Ninh Binh

    A place with big mountains and caves you can explore, and fields of green rice that look like a painting.

  • Province

    Yen Bai

    A place in the mountains where you can see lots of steps in the hills where people grow rice.
